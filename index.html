Instructions:

Use the "Add Test Title" input and button to give your test a title

Use the "Question Form" and submit button to add a question and corresponding answer choices to your test. The generator will automatically randomize the answer choices for you

To change your test title, enter a new title into the "Add Test Title" input and click the button

To delete your title, use the delete button next to the title

To delete questions from your test, use the delete buttons next to each question
